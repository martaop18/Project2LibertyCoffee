We launched in 2014 as a market stall on Leather Lane, a historic London market founded in the 1600’s – we were the first street food trader to accept card payments with no minimum and no additional charge. It’s strange to think that this was not the norm at the time. We now have seven locations, many of which are cashless. We respect history, and embrace future progression.

Handcrafted fresh doughnuts with real ingredients to deliver real flavour.

Liberty is a scratch bakery. We roll our dough by hand. We make all of our jams, compotes, fillings, glazes and toppings in-house, fresh every day, using the finest ingredients we can find to deliver real, honest flavour. It’s a lot of extra work, but we believe the results speak for themselves. Quality should never be compromised no matter the quantity, although both can exist at the same time. We think our doughnuts pair perfectly to specialty coffee, which is why we serve them together where we can. We call this #craftbyliberty
